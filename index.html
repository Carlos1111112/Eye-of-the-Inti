About Intinauta

Pioneering Peru's Space Future

Founded in 2018, Intinauta is Peru's premier space research and technology company. Named after Inti, the Incan sun god, we harness the power of space observation to illuminate the path forward for our nation.

Our mission is to provide Peruvian institutions, researchers, and businesses with world-class satellite imagery and geospatial intelligence, enabling data-driven decisions for sustainable development.
